# US Airline Market Analysis (1993–2024)

**Author:** Zakarias Musa  
**Tools:** Python, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib  

---

## Project Summary

This project performs an exploratory data analysis (EDA) on 30+ years of U.S. airline market data to uncover pricing trends, passenger demand patterns, and competition dynamics.

The goal was to transform raw airline data into clear, data-driven business insights.

---

## What Was Done

- Cleaned and validated large historical datasets  
- Analyzed fare trends over time (1993–2024)  
- Examined Fare vs Distance relationships  
- Studied Passenger Volume vs Pricing behavior  
- Evaluated the impact of market competition on fares  
- Conducted long-term trend analysis  

---

## Special Techniques Used

- **IQR (Interquartile Range) method** for robust outlier detection  
- Statistical summaries for skewed distributions  
- Time-series trend analysis  
- Business-focused data storytelling  
- Insight extraction beyond surface-level visualization  

---

## Outcome

The analysis highlights structural shifts in airline pricing, the role of competition in fare stability, and how demand and distance influence market behavior.

This project demonstrates strong skills in EDA, statistical reasoning, and translating data into strategic insights.
